About Me

I am Dr. Puspanjali Mohapatra, an assistant professor in the Department of CSE at IIIT Bhubaneswar. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science Engineering from Siksha O Anusandhan University, Bhubaneswar, and a M.Tech in Computer Science from Utkal University, Bhubaneswar. I have 9 patents, 21 publications in reputed journals, 3 publications in book chapters, and 17 publications in conferences to my name. My research interests include bioinformatics, data mining, soft computing, artificial intelligence, machine learning, pattern recognition, evolutionary computing techniques, and recommender systems. I have had the privilege of supervising 4 Ph.D. scholars, 12 M.Tech students, and 18 B.Tech students. I have taught various courses such as recommender systems, programming in C, data structures and algorithms, systems programming, computer architecture, principles of soft computing, data warehouse and data mining, advanced computer architecture, and artificial intelligence. I am fluent in English, Hindi, and Oriya.
